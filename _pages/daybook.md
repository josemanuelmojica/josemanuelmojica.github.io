The daybook contains daily observations, brief thoughts, and moments worth capturing. These are shorter entries that document the small details and passing reflections that make up daily life.

Think of this as a digital commonplace book - a place for collecting quotes, observations, fleeting thoughts, and the small moments that might otherwise be forgotten but deserve to be remembered.
