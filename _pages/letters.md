This section contains correspondence and open letters - writings addressed to specific people, groups, or the world at large. These pieces take the form of letters, whether they are actually sent or remain as thoughtful expressions directed toward particular audiences.

Letters can be personal, political, philosophical, or practical. They represent a more direct form of communication, with a clear sense of addressee and purpose.
